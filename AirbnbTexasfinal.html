INTRODUCTION

link for the Airbnb data Airbnb is an online platform that facilitates the booking and rental of lodging, primarily homes or apartments, on a short-term basis. Founded in 2008, Airbnb has grown into a global marketplace that connects people looking to rent their homes with travelers seeking unique and often more affordable accommodations than traditional hotels. The platform allows hosts to list their properties, providing details about the space, amenities, and pricing, while guests can browse listings and book accommodations for a specified period. I’ve heard many good things about this business but have not seen a market study. Today, I decided to work with this data (Airbnb property data from Texas from 2008 to 2017). This dataset includes the average night price, number of bedrooms, location, the year listed, and description, which aligns with my interest in studying the market. At the end of our analyses, we will be able to respond to multiple questions such as how many listings is there in Texas, how Airbnb has grown in Texas from 2008 to 2017, what city is the most popular with Airbnb; what is the average per night, per year, per bedroom; what number of the bedroom is the most popular? Through our analysis, we will find approaches to our findings. Our data will give a visualization and give people an idea of this business.
